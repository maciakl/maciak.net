Welcome to maciak.net

My name is Lukasz Grzegorz Maciak, but you can call me Luke for short. Yes, feel free to do the obligatory Darth Vader impersonation. No, you are not the first person who quoted that memorable Star Wars line at me, and definitely not the last one.

I am a technology blogger, a programmer and a systems administrator. I also teach "Fluency in Technology" at Montclair State University as an adjunct professor.

The most web-visible of my activities is probably my blog Terminally Incoherent which does seem to have a small but loyal readership. There are few links to some of my past and current projects in the sidebar. Check out the Publications tab to check out my academic work.
